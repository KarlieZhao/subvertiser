Trouble Shooting

We use the marker-based web AR technology to identify advertisement panels and displays at bus stops, subway stations and elsewhere on the streets. To let the artworks show up, you have to include two markers (usually at two corners of the ad panels) in your camera and hold your phone still. Marker-based AR is not so ideal in terms of user's experience, but we are trying to make it better.

  • Avoid turning your phone from landscape to portrait mode or vice versa.

  • Stay closer to the markers.

  • If you are an iOS user and browsing our page on Chrome:
    Sometimes it happens that the camera permission doesn't show up on the browser, therefore, the camera cannot be detected by our program. For now, try Safari/Firefox.

  • Find an issue not mentioned above? Let us know!
