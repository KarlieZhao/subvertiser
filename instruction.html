Trouble Shooting

  1. Avoid rotating your phone from landscape to portrait mode, or vice versa.
  2. Try not to stay too far from the marker.
  3. Chrome on ios has an issue that sometimes it can't receive the camera permission prompt, so the camera cannot be used. As an alternative for now, try Safari.
  4. Find an issue not mentioned above? Let us know!
